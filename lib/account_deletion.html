Account Deletion - VahanServ

This page provides instructions on how to delete your VahanServ user account and all associated data.

1. Deleting Your Account In-App

The simplest way to delete your account is directly through the app's settings. This will immediately and permanently delete your user profile and all personal data linked to your account.

  • Open the VahanServ app.
  • Navigate to the **Settings** or **Profile** section.
  • Tap on the **"Delete Account"** option.
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the deletion.

Note: Deleting your account is a permanent action and cannot be undone. All personal data, including your phone number, email, and photos, will be erased from our systems.

2. Requesting Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access your app or have already uninstalled it, you can request account deletion by sending us an email. Please use the email address associated with your VahanServ account to help us verify your identity.

Please send your request to:
vahanserv2023@gmail.com

3. Data We Delete

Upon a successful deletion request, we will permanently remove all your personal data, including:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Any photos uploaded to the app.
  • All service history and associated data.

We aim to process all email deletion requests within 30 days.
